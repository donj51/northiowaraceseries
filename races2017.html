Run with the River 5K

The next race is August 19, 2017 in Rock Falls, Iowa.

Wilkinson Pioneer Park in Rock Falls, IA.

The race route is through the scenic Wilkinson Pioneer Park on the trail that runs along the Shellrock River and the historic Rock Falls Covered Bridge.

Packet pick-up at the Rock Falls Community Center at 3 N Nottingham St, Rock Falls IA on Friday August 18th from 4-7 p.m. and at the park shelter house Saturday morning before the race at 7 a.m.

After the race, runners are encouraged to join the Rock Falls Fun Days celebration at the Community Center. This race will be chip timed by True Time Racing Services. Must be registered before 8/1/17 to receive a T shirt.

Aug 26 - Restoration Run Beeds Lake State Park 5k, Hampton, IA

5k race route will provide a scenic, flat tour of Beeds Lake State Park. Race begins at 8:30 a.m. Add $5 for same day registration.

All run/walk participants will receive a shirt and FREE BREAKFAST. Entry fees are $20 for the 5K. Registration and packet pick up will be August 25th from 4-7 PM. Race day registration and packet pick up is 6:30-8:15 AM at Beeds Lake State Park.

Trophies will be awarded to the top male and female finishers and medals to the top three in each age category for the 5K. Top male and female race finishers will receive free registration for 2017!
